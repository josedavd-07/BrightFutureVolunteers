The Problem

Many children cannot study properly because they don’t have basic school supplies.

In Colombia, thousands of children go to school without notebooks, pencils, or even proper uniforms. This lack of resources makes learning more difficult and discourages students from attending classes.

If children don’t receive the necessary support, they may drop out of school and have fewer opportunities in the future. Education is a fundamental right, and helping students access it is essential.
